# DLC Packs System

**Status:** Draft - Post-Launch Monetization
**Date:** 2025-01-09
**Purpose:** Paid content packs for additional characters, blueprints, and minion patterns

---

## Overview

DLC Packs are **optional paid content expansions** that add new gameplay options without fragmenting the player base. Each pack includes a themed collection of characters, weapon/item blueprints, and minion patterns.

**Key Philosophy:**
- âœ… **NOT pay-to-win** - DLC content is sidegrades, not upgrades
- âœ… **NOT required** - Base game is complete without DLC
- âœ… **Cosmetic variety + build diversity** - New playstyles, not raw power
- âœ… **Fair pricing** - $1.99-$4.99 per pack

**Brotato comparison:**
âœ… Similar model - Brotato DLC "Abyssal Terrors" is $2.99
âœ… Adds 15 characters, 21 enemies, 1 zone (content expansion, not P2W)

## DLC Pack Structure

### Standard DLC Pack Contents

**What each pack includes:**
1. **2-4 Characters** - New character types with unique mechanics
2. **5-10 Blueprints** - Weapon/item crafting recipes
3. **2-3 Minion Patterns** - Exclusive minion types (Subscription feature)
4. **Themed Content** - Cohesive theme (Energy Weapons, Medieval Knights, etc.)

**What DLC does NOT include:**
- âŒ Stat boosts (not P2W)
- âŒ Premium/Subscription tier upgrades (separate purchases)
- âŒ Exclusive shop access (everyone uses same shops)
- âŒ Gameplay advantages (balanced against base content)

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Small Pack ($1.99)
**Contents:**
- 2 characters
- 3 weapon blueprints
- 1 minion pattern

**Target:** Light DLC buyers, thematic fans
**Example:** "Speedster Pack" (2 speed-focused characters, 3 mobility weapons)

---

### Tier 2: Standard Pack ($2.99)
**Contents:**
- 3 characters
- 6 weapon/item blueprints
- 2 minion patterns

**Target:** Engaged players, collectors
**Example:** "Energy Weapons Pack" (3 tech characters, 6 energy weapons, 2 tech minions)

---

### Tier 3: Deluxe Pack ($4.99)
**Contents:**
- 4 characters
- 10 weapon/item blueprints
- 3 minion patterns
- Exclusive cosmetic (character skin or weapon skin)

**Target:** Completionists, super fans
**Example:** "Ultimate Warfare Pack" (4 military characters, 10 weapons, 3 combat minions, exclusive camo skins)

**Free Tier:**

**Premium Tier:**
- âœ… Can purchase DLC packs

**Subscription Tier:**
- âœ… Can purchase DLC packs

- Prevents DLC from being entry point (Premium is entry point)

## DLC Character Balance

### Design Principles

**DLC characters should:**
- âœ… Offer **new playstyles**, not stronger stats
- âœ… Have **unique mechanics** not in base game
- âœ… Be **balanced against base characters**
- âœ… Create **build synergies** with DLC blueprints

**Example: Technomancer (DLC character)**
```
Base Stats:
- Max HP: 100 (same as default)
- Damage: 0 (same as default)
- Engineering: +50 (unique focus)

Unique Mechanic:
- Energy weapons scale 100% with Engineering
- Minions gain +25% attack speed per 10 Engineering

Trade-off:
- Can't use non-energy weapons effectively
- Requires Engineering build (limits variety)

Result: Different playstyle, not stronger overall
```

---

### DLC vs Base Power Comparison

**Power Budget:**
```
Base Character (Bruiser):
  +20 Max HP, +15 Melee Damage
  Power Budget: ~35 stat points

DLC Character (Technomancer):
  +50 Engineering, +100% Energy weapon scaling
  Power Budget: ~35 stat points equivalent

Result: Equal power, different focus
```

---

## DLC Blueprint Balance

### Design Principles

**DLC blueprints should:**
- âœ… Offer **thematic variety**, not power creep
- âœ… Be **sidegrades** to base game blueprints
- âœ… Create **new build paths**, not mandatory upgrades
- âœ… Synergize with DLC characters (but work for all characters)

**Example: Plasma Rifle (DLC T4 Weapon)**
```
Stats:
- Damage: 50 (100% Elemental)
- Attack Speed: 1.5s cooldown
- Range: 500
- Special: Pierces 2 enemies, scales 100% with Engineering

Comparison to Base T4 Weapon (Laser Gun):
- Laser Gun: 40 damage (400% Ranged), 1.98s cooldown, pierces 1
- Plasma Rifle: 50 damage (100% Elemental), 1.5s cooldown, pierces 2

Trade-off:
- Plasma Rifle: Better for Engineering builds (Technomancer)
- Laser Gun: Better for Ranged builds (Ranger)

Result: Different builds, not strictly better
```

## DLC Content Creation Process

### Character Design Checklist

**For each DLC character:**
1. **Unique mechanic** - Not in base game
2. **Thematic cohesion** - Fits DLC pack theme
3. **Power budget** - Balanced vs base characters
4. **Build synergy** - Works with DLC blueprints
5. **Visual distinction** - Unique character model/sprite
6. **Unlock flow** - Instant unlock on DLC purchase

---

### Blueprint Design Checklist

**For each DLC blueprint:**
1. **Thematic fit** - Matches DLC pack theme
2. **Power balance** - Sidegrade, not upgrade
3. **Build enabler** - Creates new build paths
4. **Rarity distribution** - Mix of T1/T2/T3/T4
5. **Crafting cost** - Balanced vs base blueprints
6. **Instant unlock** - Added to Blueprint Library on purchase

---

### Minion Pattern Checklist

**For each DLC minion:**
1. **Unique behavior** - Different from base minions
2. **Thematic fit** - Matches DLC pack theme
3. **Subscription integration** - Works with Minion Fabricator
4. **Power balance** - Balanced vs base minions
5. **Visual distinction** - Unique minion sprite
